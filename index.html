I am particularly interested in building secure, scalable, and resilient network architectures that support business continuity and operational efficiency. My goal is to advance as a network and cybersecurity professional by contributing to technically challenging environments, strengthening infrastructure reliability, and staying aligned with modern enterprise networking and security standards.
